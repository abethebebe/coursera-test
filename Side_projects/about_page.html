Welcome to Camp Abethebebe

This Camp is for kids with austim. Everyone is welcome from anywhere in the spectrum

The term autism was first used by Leo Kanenr and Hans Asperger in the 1940s to decribe indivudal children without obvious signs of focal cerebral disease who display such symptoms as severly imparied social interaction, a bizarre and narrow range of interests language.

The disorder is now refered to as autism spectrum disorder(ASD) to include children with either mild or severe symptoms.Other rare, Very severe disorders included in the autism spectrum are Rett syndrome, which affects mainly females and childhood disintegrative disorder, which affects mainly males. If a child symptoms do not meet the specific criteria for ASD, the diagnosis is pervasive developmental disorder not other specificed.
